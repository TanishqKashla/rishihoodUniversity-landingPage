In.culcate

A digital Indian history storytelling app

Four of our first-year students built in.culcate, a digital storytelling app, that makes Indian history engaging through short byte-sized content. The startup won first prize at IIT Roorkee and is currently incubating at ISB Hyderabad by ISB Hyderabad.

Bocadoh

India's first fruit-flavoured coconut chips!

Three first-year students founded BOCADO, a healthy fruit-flavoured coconut chips brand. The founders went through 4+ months of R&D, worked with 20-30 vendors, and experimented with 7-8 product variants.

The duration of Rishihood University’s Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) Residential Program is 4 years.
The BBA course tuition fee at Rishihood University is Rs. 4.2 Lakh per year.

Yes, we have scholarships available in 4 categories.

Vidya Shree: Merit-based scholarships given to top scorers of entrance exams.

Next-Gen Women Leader: Open to meritorious young women applying for undergraduate programs who demonstrate academic excellence, leadership potential, or financial need.

Pragya Jyoti: Another Merit-based scholarship offered to needy students with excellent academic records.

Vividha Samman: A scholarship that gives a platform to talented students in design, visual and fine arts, sports or literature.

The 4-year structure is designed to make you workplace-ready by graduation, so you don't need to pursue an MBA, saving you two extra years.

One full entrepreneurship semester is dedicated to building your startup or working as an Entrepreneur-in-Residence, giving you hands-on experience.

You can choose career-focused minors such as Computer Science or Finance, which are not feasible within a 3-year curriculum.

A 4-year undergraduate degree is the internationally recognized standard and is now recommended by India's National Education Policy (NEP).

For those who wish to exit early, the program allows a UGC-recognized BBA degree after three years, providing flexibility without compromising academic value.

Students require a minimum of 60% or 6 CGPA in Grade 12 or equivalent qualifying examination from any recognized board.

Students must submit any of the following aptitude test scores: RSAT / SAT / ACT / CUET.

*Students can directly apply through their SAT scores. Rishihood University's DI Code for SAT applicants is 9208. All applicants applying with the SAT score need to upload their official transcripts on the Rishihood Application Portal.

The Rishihood Scholarship & Aptitude Test (RSAT) is Rishihood’s entrance and scholarship exam assessing aptitude, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. The test involves a video question and another section on Quantitative Aptitude and Behavioural Assessment.
